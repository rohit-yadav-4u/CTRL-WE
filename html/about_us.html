Who We Are

KalaYatra is more than just a platform — it’s a movement to revive, protect, and celebrate the soul of India through its traditional arts. Rooted in respect for heritage and driven by a love for artistic expression, we serve as a bridge between past and present, between artisans and audiences, and between tradition and innovation.We believe that every region, every community, and every art form carries a story — of devotion,identity, and history.
