Handicrafts are an integral part of India's rich cultural heritage, reflecting centuries of tradition, artistry, and craftsmanship. From intricate textiles and vibrant pottery to exquisite woodwork and metal crafts, each region of India boasts its own unique styles and techniques.

There are many types of handicrafts, each showcasing unique skills and traditions. Popular categories include textiles such as weaving and embroidery, pottery and ceramics, woodwork, metal crafts, jewelry making, basketry, leatherwork, and stone carving.
